Bread Making Ingredients

In addition to love those are some of the most used ingredients we use to make bread.

Flour

Usually strong flours are used in bread making. Wholewheat flours have lesser concentration of gluten as the bran content is increased.

Water

Water is the most commonly used liquid in bread making. It moistens the flour and helps in forming the dough. It also aids in the baking process.

Yeast

Yeast is a single cell microorganism which causes the leavening in the dough.

Milk

It makes the bread whiter and softer, and provides moisture and a distinct flavour.
